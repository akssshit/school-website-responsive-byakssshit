Frequently Asked Questions

What classes are admissions open for?

Admissions are open for Nursery to Class 10.

How can parents apply for admission?

Parents are requested to visit the school office during working hours.

Is online admission available?

Currently, admissions are processed offline at the school campus.

What is the admission process?

The process includes enquiry & registration, interaction/assessment, document verification, and confirmation of admission.

Are there any age criteria for different classes?

Yes, the age criteria vary for each class. Parents are requested to contact the school office for exact details.

How can I know about the fee structure?

The detailed fee structure is available at the school office. Parents are advised to visit for complete information.

Is transport facility available?

The school provides bus facility for certain routes. Parents can inquire at the office.
