About Us

St. John's School Chakia is a co-educational English-medium school located in Professor Colony Lane, Ward 9, Bara Chakia, East Champaran, Bihar. The school provides quality education from the foundational years through higher classes, with a focus on academic excellence, holistic development, and character building. Managed by the Sumitra Devi Nagina Prasad Educational Trust, the institution strives to create a nurturing learning environment supported by experienced teachers and a student-centric approach. Emphasis is placed on strong communication skills, disciplined study habits, and extracurricular engagement to prepare students for future challenges. The school's curriculum and teaching methods are designed to help all students achieve their full potential while fostering confidence and leadership qualities. Parents and the local community recognize the school for its dedicated faculty, supportive environment, and commitment to overall growth of students.
